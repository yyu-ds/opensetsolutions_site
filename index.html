02 — About

Small by design, on purpose.

Open Set Solutions LLC is a boutique technology and AI consultancy. We take on a handful of engagements at a time so the people you meet are the people who do the work.

The name comes from topology. An open set is a region where every point has breathing room inside its neighborhood — the boundary stays open, nothing is pinned to a hard edge. We think good systems should feel the same way: room to adapt as you learn, boundaries that flex instead of crack, and space for your team to grow into.

That belief shapes how we work — careful, senior, and unhurried, with an eye on what your system needs to become, not just what it needs today.

  • SeniorNo hand-offs to junior teams. You work with the people who decide and build.
  • OpenNo lock-in or black boxes. We leave you with systems your team can own.
  • HonestWe'll tell you when AI isn't the answer. The advice is the product.
